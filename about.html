Common questions

FAQ

How does M-Pesa payment work on PixelCart? +
At checkout, you enter your Safaricom number. We send an STK Push prompt to your phone — just enter your M-Pesa PIN and the payment is confirmed instantly. Your order is placed as soon as we receive confirmation from Safaricom's Daraja API.
Do all laptops come with a warranty? +
Yes. All laptops sold on PixelCart come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. We only stock from authorized distributors so your warranty is always valid.
How long does delivery take? +
Within Nairobi CBD and Westlands — same day delivery. Other Nairobi areas — next business day. Outside Nairobi — 2–3 business days via courier.
Can I return a laptop if it has issues? +
Yes — we have a 7-day return policy for hardware defects. If your laptop has an issue that isn't user-caused, contact us and we'll arrange a pickup and replacement or refund.
Do you offer bulk / corporate pricing? +
Absolutely. For orders of 5+ units, contact us directly via WhatsApp or email for special pricing and flexible payment arrangements.
